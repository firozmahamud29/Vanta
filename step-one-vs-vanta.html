FAQ

Common Questions

Is VANTA better than Step One for running?
For running-specific use, VANTA Sweat Series has the stronger performance-fabric angle, while Step One has a credible anti-chafe story and comfort-focused bamboo construction.
Is bamboo underwear good for running?
Bamboo underwear is soft and breathable, but it can retain more moisture during high-sweat activity than quick-dry performance fabrics.
What is the best underwear for sweating?
Quick-dry, moisture-wicking fabrics are usually best for sweating because they move moisture away from the skin and dry faster.
How do I stop underwear riding up when running?
Choose underwear with a stable fit, supportive structure, and fabric designed to stay in place during movement.
