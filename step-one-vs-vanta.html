Activity Analysis

Which Is Better for Running?

When running, underwear needs to stay in place, dry quickly, reduce friction, support movement, and avoid bunching. This is where VANTA Sweat Series stands out. The lightweight performance fabric is better suited to cardio, long walks, hikes, gym sessions, and hot Australian weather. Step One can work for light exercise, but bamboo fabric generally holds more moisture once heavily sweated in.

Winner: VANTA

Comfort & Support

Which Is Better for Chafing & Support?

Chafing Verdict

Both brands help reduce chafing, but in different ways. Step One uses inner thigh glide panels. VANTA focuses more on fit, leg stability, and keeping moisture lower. For many people, moisture build-up causes friction more than fabric alone.

  • Thigh rubbing focus: Step One
  • Sweat + movement focus: VANTA

Support Verdict

Support matters for walking, sport, commuting, and all-day comfort. VANTA uses a pouch-focused design that gives more separation and room than standard flat-front underwear. This often feels better during movement and long wear.

Winner: VANTA
