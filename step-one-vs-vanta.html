The Verdict

Step One and VANTA are both leaders in men's underwear, but they serve different purposes. If you need something for the office or casual daily wear, Step One's bamboo feel is hard to beat.

However, if you are a runner or hit the gym hard, the moisture retention of bamboo becomes a liability. VANTA was engineered specifically for the man who sweats. It stays light while Step One gets heavy. It holds its shape while Step One can stretch once damp. For performance, VANTA is the clear winner.
