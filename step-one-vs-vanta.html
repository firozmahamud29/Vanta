Which is the Professional Choice for Running?

For running, the primary metric is moisture management. Bamboo, while extremely soft, holds moisture once saturated. VANTA's synthetic performance blend is designed to move moisture away from the skin and evaporate it instantly. This keeps the fabric lightweight and prevents the sag that often occurs with wet natural fibers.

Verdict for Athletes: VANTA wins on weight, drying speed, and structural integrity under sweat load.
