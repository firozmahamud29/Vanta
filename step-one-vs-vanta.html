Which Is Better for Running?

When running, you need fabric that dries quickly and stays in place. This is where VANTA Sweat Series stands out. The lightweight performance fabric is better suited to cardio, long walks, hikes, and hot Australian weather.

Winner: VANTA

Which Is Better for Chafing?

Step One uses inner thigh glide panels, while VANTA focuses more on fit, leg stability, and keeping moisture lower. Since moisture build-up often causes friction more than fabric alone, VANTA has the edge for heavy sweat.

Which Is Better for Support?

VANTA uses a pouch-focused design that gives more separation and room than standard flat-front underwear. This often feels better during movement and long wear.

Winner: VANTA
