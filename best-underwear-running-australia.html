Essential Criteria

What Actually Matters for Running

Cotton and soft lounge fabrics often absorb sweat and become heavier. For running, you need quick-drying materials, moisture-wicking fabrics, and lightweight construction.

Fit & Stability

If your underwear moves or bunches, you’ll notice it quickly. Look for stretch that holds shape and secure leg openings.

Support

Support helps reduce friction and excess movement. A well-designed pouch or contour fit can make a noticeable difference.
