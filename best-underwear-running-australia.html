FAQ Deep Dive

Runner Intelligence

The best running underwear should dry quickly, stay in place during movement, and reduce friction during longer sessions.
Lightweight quick-dry fabrics are often the best choice for warm conditions because they stay cooler and dry faster than heavier absorbent fabrics.
Cotton can feel comfortable casually, but for intense runs many people prefer faster-drying technical fabrics.
A secure fit, supportive cut and stable leg design usually help reduce ride-up.
Bamboo can feel soft and breathable, but often retains more moisture than technical performance fabrics during heavy sweat.
